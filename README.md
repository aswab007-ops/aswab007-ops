### Carmandi Car Inspection App

A professional Flutter app for vehicle inspectors to capture structured inspection reports, photos, damage maps, and final submissions.

Highlights:

- Step-by-step mobile inspection workflow
- Camera and gallery upload support
- Vehicle metadata and condition capture
- Exterior damage mapping
- API-based report submission
- Retry-friendly inspection flow
